# __P2g__

_Welcome to P2g - the api which aims to provide a light-touch upon the world of lifestyle. Instead of the conventional multi-medial content, P2g seeks to establish more of a conservative platform where users are given simple tools and digital space for:_
* ___sharing their life paradigm through the lens of diary-like entries;___
* ___a unique exchange of ideas, facilitating delivery of an individual's tastes and preferences - where liking is not a matter of a numeric increment - but the act of carefully tought-through process, via P2g platform bringing resonance to people's creative endeavours;
* ___establishing a meet-up-point for  those individuals willing to develop thier writing skills by getting a genuine feedback through impressions of those users attending their feeds;___

<br>

>>>User story: "I wish to access a closed community-based platform which can provide me with a registration and data protection. As a I user, I wish to have the ability to share my life's interests with others who might be in a search of an authentic lifestyle. I further seek to get an easy navigation across needs of: making updates to my profile and posts, following up the community around my feeds and have an access to other users' feeds."
